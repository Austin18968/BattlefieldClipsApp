# Battlefield Clips App

## Overview
The Battlefield Clips App is a mobile platform designed for fans of Battlefield 6 to share, vote, and enjoy the most epic in-game moments. Users can upload video clips of their gameplay, vote for the clip of the week, and explore other clips in a user-friendly interface.

## Features
- **Clip Upload:** Safely upload your crazy Battlefield 6 moments.
- **Voting System:** Weekly voting for the best clip.
- **Video Playback:** Seamlessly explore and play videos.
- **Navigation:** Intuitive navigation with a "Back to Homepage" button.

## How to Use
1. Install the app on your phone (to be implemented).
2. Sign up or log in to your account.
3. Upload your clips via the "Upload" section.
4. Explore and vote on other clips in the "Weekly Voting" section!

## Technology Stack
- Mobile Framework: React Native
- Backend: Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage)
- Video Player: React Native Video
